Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I register for a competition?

To register: click "Join Competition", fill out the registration form and complete any required payments. You'll receive a confirmation email when registration is successful.

How do I submit my entry?

After registering, go to the "Submit" page, choose the appropriate competition round, and upload your submission files according to the guidelines.

Judging & Results

How long does the review process take?

Our expert judges typically complete reviews within 2-4 weeks after the submission deadline. You'll be notified when your feedback is available.

What do winners receive?

Winners receive certificates, the opportunity to be featured, and public recognition. Prize details are listed on each competition page.
