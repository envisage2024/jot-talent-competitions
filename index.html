Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enter a competition?

To enter a competition, first create an account on our platform. Then navigate to the Competitions page, select the competition you're interested in, and follow the submission instructions. You'll need to complete the entry form and upload your submission before the deadline.

What are the judging criteria?

Judging criteria vary by competition but generally include creativity, originality, technical skill, adherence to the theme or guidelines, and overall impact. Specific criteria for each competition are detailed on the competition information page.

When will results be announced?

Results are typically announced within 4-6 weeks after the submission deadline. All participants will be notified via email when results are available, and winners will be announced on our website and social media channels.

Can I submit previously published work?

This depends on the specific competition. Some competitions accept previously published work, while others require original, unpublished material. Please check the rules for each competition before submitting.

How are prizes distributed?

Prize money is typically distributed via electronic transfer within 30 days of the winner announcement. Other prizes may have different distribution methods which will be detailed in the winner notification.

Do you offer feedback on submissions?

We provide detailed feedback on all submissions for competitions that include this feature. When feedback is offered, it will be accessible through your account dashboard after results are announced.
